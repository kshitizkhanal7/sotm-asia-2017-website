Program Schedule

State of the Map Asia 2017 is going to take place on September 23 - 24, 2017 in Kathmandu, Nepal.

Please propose your session in this call for proposals. The sessions proposed can be one of the three:

  • Lightning talk (10 minutes each)
  • Presentations (15 minutes each)
  • Breakout session, Workshop format (1 or 2 hours each)
  • Country presentation on State of the Map (20 minutes each. A guideline will be provided.)


Venue

Heritage Garden, Lalitpur, Nepal
