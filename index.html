State of the Map Asia is an event for all mappers and OpenStreetMap users. Enjoy two days in Nepal with talks, discussions, and workshops all around the free and open map of the world.


This is also an opportunity to acknowledge the contributions of OSM mappers in the aftermath of Nepal Earthquake 2015 from all over the world and inform how OSM has been used in disaster management and development in Nepal.





When? September 23 - 24, 2017

Where?Heritage Garden
Lalitpur, Nepal

Hosted ByKathmandu Living Labs and OSM Community of Nepal
